Knowledge Management

Checksheets

Checksheets allows you to create, manage and reuse your organization’s knowledge. Problems that are encountered in e.g. Visual Planning and Pulse is followed up with actions or solutions. These learnings are then documented as checksheets – in that way, you avoid make the same mistake twice and knowledge will remain with the organization.

Checksheets combines A3 Guidelines and checklists with a focus on “Know what” to do “How” it should be done, and “Why” we need to do it.  It’s easy to upload photos, trade-off curves or additional text to explain the check items. The documents are interactive and web-based which facilitates users ‘on the ground’. The only thing you need to access and edit the Checksheets is a web browser.

Checksheets main features:

· Know What, How and Why
· Focuses on knowledge reuse
· Easy to use and update
