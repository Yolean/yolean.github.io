🎉 New
 * New columns in Excel export for "Created by" and individual posts.
 * Excel export available from the filter menu on the board.

🍰 Enhancements
 * For users of email subscriptions in projects with more than one meeting board for Design it's now possible to, alongside which discipline(s), choose which meeting board you wish to subscribe to.
 * Integrations has gotten a facelift to improve the usability.


🐛 Fixes
 * Milstone codes can now be edited.
 * If you're using Yolean Design on a site with a lot of projects, the saving times have been reduced by a lot.
