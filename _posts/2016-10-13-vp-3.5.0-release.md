Based on our ranking of user "pains" and "gains" (both of which we have plenty) we made navigation the focus of VP 2017.
Navigation means three things:
 * Zooming in and out on the X axis, i.e. time.
 * Zooming in on rows to see more task details.
 * Board-hopping, like when you go through your initiatives or back and forth between My Commitments and your group's board(s).

Release early, release often - but only internally this time.
After a mere week of coding this is clearly alpha, so dear users don't worry you won't get the upgrade.
We use it for all our in-house visual planning though.
