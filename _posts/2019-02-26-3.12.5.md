🎉 New

🍰 Enhancements
 * It is now possible to turn off the functionality that opens boards on the date of the earliest open note.

🐛 Fixes
 * Fixed an issue where browsers would, on rare occasions, stop receiving changes in the board after establishing a new internet connection
 * It was able to move milestones before they were properly saved after being created. This could lead to UI issues on slow connections.
